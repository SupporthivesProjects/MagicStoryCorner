How it works

Every great story starts with a spark of imagination. Whether you’re creating your own adventure or exploring our story library, LittleStoryBox makes storytelling magical and effortless. Follow these simple steps to bring your tale to life.

Step 1

Pick your path

Decide how you’d like to begin. If you want something quick and ready, browse our catalogue of magical tales and jump straight in. If you’d rather create something unique, choose the custom flow where you can shape every part of the story from scratch.

Step 2

Choose your heroes

If you opted for a custom story, then you’ll need a hero! Add your child, family, or friends as the main characters with their names, roles, and traits. You can even upload an avatar or photo to make the adventure feel even more personal.

Step 3

Shape the world

Build the stage for your adventure. Pick from enchanted forests, pirate seas, outer space, or cosy bedtime settings. Decide the type of plot, whether it is a silly quest or a heartwarming tale, and set the mood that makes it just right for the moment.

Step 4

Bring it to life

Once your path is set, the magic begins. Add voices, pick styles, and let the story unfold with your unique touches. Preview the final adventure, save it to your library, and share it with friends and family so the tale lives on.
